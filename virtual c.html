How it Works

  1. Schedule a virtual consultation through our website or mobile app
  2. Receive a confirmation email with a link to join the consultation
  3. Join the consultation via video conferencing and speak with our healthcare professional
  4. Discuss your symptoms, medical history, and any concerns you may have
  5. Receive a diagnosis and treatment plan, if necessary
